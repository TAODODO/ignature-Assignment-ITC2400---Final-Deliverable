About Us

Learn more about the story, mission, and team behind IKUN Photo.

Our Story

This website is created for photography enthusiasts. It is a simple and user-friendly platform where anyone can upload and share their photos. Our goal is to enable users to easily showcase their creativity and learn from each other.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a friendly and open photo-sharing community. Everyone is welcome to join, post photos and enjoy others' works. We believe that sharing photos helps people build connections and improve skills.

Our Team

We are a small student team and designing this website is part of a school project. Each member is responsible for different aspects, such as design, coding and layout. We all love art and photography.

Why Choose Us

Our website is simple and easy to use, completely free, and accessible to everyone. You can post photos at any time, browse the portfolios of other users and draw inspiration from them. This is a simple, secure and enjoyable space where you can share the wonderful moments captured in your lens.
