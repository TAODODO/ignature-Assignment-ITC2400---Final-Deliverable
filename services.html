Support Services

We provide a range of support services to make your photography experience smooth, safe, and enjoyable.

Below is an overview of the support services we offer, including schedules, locations, and how to access them.

  1. Transportation Support

    • City Photo Tour Shuttle - Sat & Sun, 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
    • Pick-up & Drop-off - From downtown station to main studio, by appointment only
    • Meeting Location - Main studio entrance, 15 minutes before departure

  2. Studio & Equipment Assistance

    • On-site Studio Assistant - Mon-Fri, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
    • Lighting Setup Help - Free support for all studio bookings
    • Equipment Check & Cleaning - Every Wednesday, 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

  3. Photography Workshops

    • Beginner Camera Basics - Every Saturday, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
    • Portrait Lighting Session - 1st and 3rd Sunday of each month
    • Max Participants - 10 people per workshop, registration required

  4. Editing & Online Support

    • Editing Help Desk - Online via Zoom, Tue & Thu, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
    • File Backup Guidance - Email support for organizing and backing up photo files
    • Turnaround Time - We respond to most online requests within 24 hours

How to Access These Services

  • Use the Contact page to register for workshops or ask questions.
  • Check your email for confirmations, schedule changes, and safety notices.
  • Arrive 10-15 minutes early for any in-person session or shuttle service.
